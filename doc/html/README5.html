5. Contributing to XFree86

If you have any new work or enhancements/bug fixes for existing work, please submit them to patch@XFree86.org or to our bug tracking system. This will ensure that they are reviewed for inclusion in future releases. New works should be discussed on our devel@XFree86.org list prior to submission.

Current information about the XFree86 development process can be found at our web site.
