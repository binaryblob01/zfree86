1. Introduction to the 4.x Release Series

XFree86 4.0 was the first official release of the XFree86 4 series. The current release (4.8.0) is the latest in that series. The XFree86 4.x series represents a significant redesign of the XFree86 X server, with a strong focus on modularity and configurability.
